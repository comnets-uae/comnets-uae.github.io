Nouar AlDahoul

Postdoctoral Associate - New York University

I am a postdoc associate researcher at NYU - Computer Science. My current research focuses on utilizing Machine Learning Techniques in Computational Social Science. I received my Ph.D. degree in machine learning from International Islamic University Malaysia. My research interests lie in Machine Learning Applications, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Generative Modelling, Internet of Things. Outside of academia, I have also been training hundreds of employees in several companies in Malaysia.
